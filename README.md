# Devo Daily — Proverbs & Psalms

> *"Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path."* — Psalm 119:105

**Devo Daily** is a simple, focused devotional app that delivers a fresh reading from the Book of **Proverbs** and the Book of **Psalms** every single day. No noise. No clutter. Just timeless wisdom and heartfelt praise, one day at a time.

---

## ✨ About the App

The Bible's Proverbs and Psalms are two of the most beloved and practical books of Scripture:

- 📖 **Proverbs** — Packed with concise, actionable wisdom for everyday life: relationships, work, integrity, speech, and more. A chapter a day through Proverbs fits perfectly into any monthly reading plan.
- 🎵 **Psalms** — 150 poems and songs that cover the full range of human emotion — joy, grief, gratitude, doubt, and hope — all directed toward God. Psalms teach us how to pray and how to praise.

**Devo Daily** keeps the devotional experience intentional and distraction-free by focusing exclusively on these two books.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 📅 **Daily Readings** — Automatically surfaces a Proverb and a Psalm reading matched to the current day.
- 🔖 **Simple Navigation** — Easily browse forward or backward through readings at your own pace.
- 🌙 **Clean, Minimal Interface** — Designed to help you focus on the Word without distractions.
- 💾 **Lightweight & Fast** — Runs smoothly without heavy dependencies or bloated libraries.

## 🙏 Purpose & Motivation

This project was built out of a desire to make daily Scripture reading simple, consistent, and accessible. By limiting the scope to Proverbs and Psalms, the app encourages a sustainable reading habit — wisdom in the morning, praise throughout the day.

Whether you're brand new to the Bible or a long-time reader looking for a structured daily rhythm, **Devo Daily** is here to help you stay grounded in God's Word.
